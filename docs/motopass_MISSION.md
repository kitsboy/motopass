# MotoPass — Mission

## Our Purpose
To make sovereign citizenship and residency-by-investment accessible, verifiable, and Bitcoin-native — enabling freedom of movement and economic opportunity for every person on Earth.

## Core Values
1. **Truth You Can Verify** — Every claim independently verifiable on Bitcoin via OpenTimestamps and Satohash.
2. **Bitcoin-Native** — All investment pathways payable in BTC.
3. **Privacy by Design** — No unnecessary data collection.
4. **Global Intelligence** — Maps visa-free corridors across 50+ countries.

## The Problem We Solve
CBI/RBI programs are opaque, lawyer-dependent, and exclude Bitcoin holders.

## Our Solution
A passport command center with Bitcoin-verifiable claims and BTC-native investment pathways.

## Who It Serves
High-net-worth individuals, digital nomads, and Bitcoin holders seeking sovereign freedom of movement.
